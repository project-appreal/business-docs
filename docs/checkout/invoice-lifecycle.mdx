# Invoice Lifecycle

Every invoice goes through a series of statuses from creation to completion.
Understanding these statuses helps you handle payments correctly and build robust integrations.

## Invoice statuses

### `PREPARED`

The invoice has been created but the customer has not yet selected a payment method.
The invoice remains in this state for up to **15 minutes** by default.

- A wallet has **not** been assigned yet
- The customer sees the payment method selection screen
- Transitions to `ACTIVE` when the customer selects a currency and network

### `ACTIVE`

The customer has selected a payment method and a blockchain wallet has been assigned.
The invoice is now waiting for the customer to send payment.

- A unique wallet address is displayed to the customer
- The payment window is **15 minutes** by default (configurable)
- The exchange rate and expected amount are locked

### `CONFIRMING`

A payment has been detected on the blockchain but has not yet reached the required number of confirmations.

- The transaction hash (`txHash`) is recorded
- Appreal monitors the blockchain for additional confirmations
- A background job checks confirmation status every **5 minutes**

### `COMPLETED`

The payment has been fully confirmed on the blockchain. This is the **final success state**.

- The merchant's account balance has been credited
- The customer is redirected to the `redirectUrl` (if provided)
- A webhook notification is sent

### `EXPIRED`

The invoice expired before a payment was received or confirmed.

- If no payment method was selected: `PREPARED` &rarr; `EXPIRED`
- If payment method was selected but no payment received: `ACTIVE` &rarr; `EXPIRED`
- The assigned wallet is released back to the pool
- Expired invoices with no activity are purged after approximately 1 year

### `CANCELLED`

The merchant cancelled the invoice before it was completed.

- Only invoices in `PREPARED` or `ACTIVE` status can be cancelled
- Use `POST /invoices/:id/cancel` with a `reason` field
- This is a **final state**

## Payment statuses

In addition to the invoice status, each invoice has a payment status that tracks the transaction:

| Payment Status | Description |
|---|---|
| `UNPAID` | No payment has been detected |
| `PROCESSING` | Payment detected, awaiting blockchain confirmations |
| `PAID` | Exact amount confirmed |
| `OVERPAID` | Customer sent more than the expected amount |
| `UNDERPAID` | Customer sent less than expected (within tolerance) |

### Key rules

- An invoice can only move **forward** through the lifecycle (no going back to a previous status)
- `COMPLETED` and `CANCELLED` are **final states**
- `EXPIRED` is final unless txHash recovery is used (see [Handling Invoice Exceptions](/checkout/handling-invoice-exceptions))
- Invoices in `CONFIRMING` status **cannot** be cancelled

## Required confirmations

All blockchain networks currently require **3 block confirmations** before a payment is considered confirmed:

| Network | Confirmations Required |
|---|---|
| Ethereum | 3 |
| Polygon | 3 |
| BNB Smart Chain | 3 |
| Arbitrum One | 3 |
| TRON | 3 |
