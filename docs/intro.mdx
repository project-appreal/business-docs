# Welcome to Appreal

The Appreal Business API enables merchants to accept cryptocurrency payments, send payouts to recipients,
and manage balances across multiple blockchain networks — all through a single REST API.

## What can you do with Appreal?

### Invoice

Create invoices and accept crypto payments from your customers. Appreal handles wallet assignment,
blockchain confirmations, and balance updates automatically.

[Get started with Invoice &rarr;](/checkout/overview)

### Payouts

Send funds to bank accounts or crypto wallets. Supports fiat-to-crypto, crypto-to-fiat,
and crypto-to-crypto transfers with real-time exchange rates.

[Get started with Payouts &rarr;](/payouts/overview)

### Reference

Explore supported blockchain networks, currencies, and exchange rate APIs.

[View supported networks &rarr;](/reference/supported-networks)
